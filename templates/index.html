About this platform

This comprehensive medical test panel is designed to assess your risk for Diabetes and Cardiovascular Diseases (CVD)—two of the most common and interconnected health conditions. It includes blood tests such as Fasting Blood Sugar, HbA1c, and Lipid Profile to evaluate blood glucose control and cholesterol levels. Additional assessments like Blood Pressure Monitoring and Electrocardiogram (ECG) help detect early signs of heart disease or hypertension. Early diagnosis through these tests allows for timely intervention, lifestyle modification, and effective treatment planning. Medi Predict is dedicated to providing the best healthcare insights.
